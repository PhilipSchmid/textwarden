TextWarden Help

Welcome to TextWarden, your privacy-first grammar checker for macOS. TextWarden checks your spelling and grammar while you type, with all processing happening locally on your Mac.

Getting Started

TextWarden works automatically in the background once you've granted Accessibility permissions. Here's what you need to know:

  • Menu Bar Icon - TextWarden lives in your menu bar. Click the icon to access settings and controls.
  • Error Indicator - When errors are found, a small indicator appears near your cursor. Click it to see suggestions.
  • Apply Corrections - Click any suggestion to apply it instantly.

Quick Tips

  • Press Cmd + Control + G to toggle grammar checking on/off
  • Press Cmd + Control + S to run a style check (requires Apple Intelligence)
  • Right-click the menu bar icon for quick access to pause/resume
  • Add words to your custom dictionary from the suggestion popover
